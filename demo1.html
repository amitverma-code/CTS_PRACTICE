CS3, the Intro computer survey class at Santa Monica College, is offered every semester.Several sections are available. Nevertheless, popularity of tha class has meant in the past that not all students wanting to take the class could be admitted.


The primary textbook for this course is Computer,Tools for and Information Age by H.L Capron
